Privacy Policy

SummerVeld Hound Resort App

Last Updated: 21/10/2025

Introduction

Welcome to the SummerVeld Hound Resort App ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our pet resort management application and related services. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Profile Information: User preferences, settings, and profile photos
  • Contact Information: Address and emergency contact details

Pet Information

  • Pet Profiles: Pet names, breeds, ages, medical information, vaccination records
  • Pet Photos: Images uploaded for pet profiles and identification
  • Pet Care Preferences: Dietary requirements, behavioral notes, special needs

App Usage Information

  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and session tokens via Firebase Auth
  • Event Participation: RSVP responses, event attendance, and comments
  • App Activity: Features used, time spent in app, and user interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers

Payment Information (Optional Virtual Adoption)

  • Payment Processing: Integration with Zapper payment gateway for virtual adoption donations
  • Transaction Data: Payment amounts, transaction IDs, and payment status
  • Billing Information: Billing address and payment method details (processed by Zapper)
  • Adoption Records: Virtual adoption selections and donation history

Technical Information

  • Device Permissions: Camera and storage access for photo uploads
  • Network Information: IP address, connection type, and network provider
  • Error Logs: App crash reports and performance data for debugging
  • Analytics Data: App usage patterns and feature utilization

How We Use Your Information

Primary App Functions

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining user accounts
  • Pet Resort Services: Managing pet profiles, bookings, and care schedules
  • Event Management: Organizing resort events and managing RSVPs
  • Communication: Sending notifications about events, bookings, and resort updates
  • Customer Support: Responding to inquiries and providing assistance

Virtual Adoption Program (Optional)

  • Payment Processing: Facilitating donations through Zapper payment gateway
  • Adoption Records: Tracking virtual adoption selections and maintaining adoption history
  • Receipt Generation: Providing payment confirmations and tax receipts
  • Program Updates: Sending information about adopted pets and resort activities

App Improvement

  • Performance Optimization: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Feature Development: Understanding user needs to develop new features
  • Bug Fixes: Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Security Monitoring: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party services that help us operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, and file storage services
  • Zapper Payment Gateway: Payment processing for virtual adoption donations
  • Google Analytics: App usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Cloud Storage Providers: Secure storage of user data and pet photos

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Respond to government requests

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosting on secure cloud platforms with regular updates

Payment Security

  • PCI Compliance: Payment processing through Zapper's PCI-compliant platform
  • No Storage: We do not store credit card information or payment details
  • Secure Transmission: All payment data is encrypted during transmission

Data Retention

Retention Periods

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 2 years after deactivation
  • Pet Information: Retained for 5 years after last activity for veterinary and care purposes
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • App Usage Data: Retained for 2 years for analytics and improvement purposes

Data Deletion

  • Account Deletion: You can request account deletion, which will remove personal information
  • Pet Data: Pet information may be retained longer if required for veterinary records
  • Payment Records: Transaction records may be retained longer for legal compliance

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • Data Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Data Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data

Communication Preferences

  • Email Notifications: Opt out of marketing emails while keeping essential notifications
  • Push Notifications: Manage notification preferences in app settings
  • Virtual Adoption Updates: Choose to receive updates about adopted pets

Payment Preferences

  • Virtual Adoption: Participation in virtual adoption program is completely optional
  • Payment Methods: Manage payment methods through Zapper's secure platform
  • Donation History: Access your donation history and receipts

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Appropriate technical and organizational measures

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
