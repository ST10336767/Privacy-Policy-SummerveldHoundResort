How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: In-App Request

  1. Open the SummerVeld Hound Resort App
  2. Go to Profile Settings
  3. Select Data Management
  4. Tap Delete My Account
  5. Follow the confirmation prompts

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to our support team with the following information:

  • Email: keatonjones02@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - SummerVeld Hound Resort App
  • Include:
  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account
